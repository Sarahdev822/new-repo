Biography

Name: Gun Atthaphan Phunsawat (กัน อรรถพันธ์)

Birthdate: October 4, 1993

Height: 170 cm

Nationality: Thai

Known For: Theory of Love, Not Me, The Gifted

Gun Atthaphan is a Thai actor and model, best known for his roles in Boys' Love (BL) dramas. He gained international fame for his performances in Theory of Love (2019) and Not Me (2021).
