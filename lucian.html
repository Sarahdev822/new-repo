Our Story

Lucian Fragrances was born in the sun-drenched hills of Provence, where founder Lucian Moreau first discovered his passion for perfumery as a child in his grandmother's lavender fields. After studying under master perfumers in Grasse, Lucian set out to create scents that capture fleeting moments of beauty and emotion.

Each Lucian fragrance is crafted using the finest natural ingredients sourced from around the world. Our signature blends combine unexpected notes to create truly unique olfactory experiences. From the citrus groves of Sicily to the spice markets of Marrakech, every bottle tells a story of its origins.

Today, Lucian remains a family-owned maison dedicated to artisan craftsmanship. We still use traditional techniques passed down through generations, while innovating with modern sustainable practices. Our perfumes are bottled by hand in our atelier, where every detail matters.
